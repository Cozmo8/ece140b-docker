Assignment #4: Product UI

Fitt's Law

The Windows 11 taskbar is located on the bottom center of the screen, so users know where to click when they want to open or navigate between apps.


Jacob's Law

Google is the most prominently used search engine, but compitetors provide a similar UI to attract the same customer demographic. For example, Microsoft Bing's search engine functions very similar to that of Google's, providing users with trending searches and a similar design.


Law of Proximity

On the Canvas website, the navigation tools and assignment listings are within close range of each other, making it easy for students to navigate between pages.
